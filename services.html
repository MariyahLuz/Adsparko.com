A Little About Us

Adsparko is a full service 360 degree advertising agency with a passion for bridging the relevance gap between brands and their audiences. We provide the full service from below-the-line, through-the -line and above-the-line communication for brands.

What We Do

Creative Design
Our team carries a couple of creative geniuses that thrive in building designs to give you a one of a kind digital presence that will achieve your goals. We use a combination of art and data, to get into the minds of your target audience, understand how they relate with the product, then create the message that will make conversions the client needs in forms like;
ideation, graphic design, script writing and animation and motion Design.
Public Relations
We understand how delicate and vital managing a brand is; Crisis management, post-strategy planning and content development is where we excel. Adsparko PR also ensures press coverage for all clientele events to position a brand in the positive light. Under this wing we offer a range of services like;
Sponsorship management, Corporate Social Responsibility Management, Government Relations (Lobbying and Advocacy), Event Planning & Management, Crisis Management.
Experiential Marketing
A solid emotional connection between any brand and their customer is a key priority for a business to flourish. We have the unbeatable expertise in creating innovative and engaging strategies for memorable customer experiences. With this an understanding is built which definitely spikes sales of the brand’s products and services. We execute these with a lot of ease;
Product sampling – Mall Activations, Bar Crawls, Product Sensitization - Rig car activations , Launch Activations - Flash Mobs.
Corporate social responsibility
CSR relates to good news stories and is a source of positive public relations. At Adsparko, our PR professionals identify worthwhile causes and mobilize corporate companies to make investment in their communities. We have a range of projects that we are currently driving and the latest is below. Feel free to follow us on our social media pages to stay informed on our CSR projects.

Event Planning and management
Empathy is our DNA. Our preference is to connect with you first and work closely together to ensure accurate message portrayal and see that we do not miss any detail on our journey to create a remarkable event that will create a great impression that will last at your clients’ heart. On interaction you will find that we’re focused on creating an event that shall sweep your audience off their feet, and we definitely are to make it happen.
Media Buying & Management
It is just not enough to have compelling visuals. Our goal as a company is to focus on the strategies behind the campaigns,give best rates for advertising material and get impressions from the right audience at the most favorable cost. Our team further helps identify the kind of media most suitable for every particular audience and the frequencies to follow so as to ensure viewership from that audience in target.
Brand ambassadorship
To awaken the emotion of your audience, we have a brand ambassador program filled with individuals of interesting and different caliber to embody your brand’s image and represent it in every suitable way. You will be thrilled to learn how dedicated they are to executing a perfect role .
Digital marketing
Navigating the digital world can be hard without the right partner. We are here with a full blown digital marketing plan to increase conversions, drive awareness and boost brand recognition. We get this done with; Content creation, Digital Strategy Formulation, Digital media buying, and Search Engine Optimization.
